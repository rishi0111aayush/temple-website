How Visitor Tracking Works

Every time someone opens the temple website (index.html), a visit is automatically recorded here.

Visits are stored locally in the browser. To track visitors across different devices and the internet, you will need web hosting.

For now, this dashboard works perfectly as a demo to show your boss the concept and layout.

Tip: Open the temple site in a new tab a few times to see the visitor count go up here.
