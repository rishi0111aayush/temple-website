🛕 Kuwee Wale Baba Ji — User Flow

How a visitor experiences the website and the temple

🌐 Phase 1 — Online
🔍

Step 1

Discover the Website

User finds the site via Google search, social media or word of mouth

📖

Step 2

Explore the Website

Reads about the temple, browses gallery, checks aarti timings & location

🗺️

Step 3

Plan the Visit

Picks an aarti slot and gets directions to reach the temple

❓

Ready to Visit Today?

User decides whether to visit now or come back later

✔ YES Proceeds to the temple
✖ NOT YET Bookmarks site & returns later
🛕 Phase 2 — At the Temple
🚗

Step 4

Arrive at the Temple

Reaches Kuwee Wale Baba Ji Mandir, Agra

👣

Step 5

Enter & Cleanse

Removes footwear, washes hands and enters with a pure heart

🪔

Step 6

Darshan & Aarti

Witnesses the sacred aarti and receives blessings of Baba Ji

Key Moment
🌸

Step 7

Offer Prayers

Lights a diya, offers flowers and seeks personal blessings

🍬

Step 8

Receive Prasad

Accepts the blessed prasad distributed to all devotees

🙏

Final Step

Leave Blessed

Departs with peace, faith and the grace of Baba Ji in heart
